Hi, I’m Raiven — the heart and hands behind Soulful Syntax.
I’m a Full Stack Web Developer, a wife, and a stay-at-home mom to three incredible little ones under the age of three (yes, my coffee is strong and my schedule is divine-led!). My journey began in Ypsilanti, Michigan, where I was born and raised, and continued through college in Chicago, Illinois, where I fell in love with both code and calling.
With a background that blends creativity, technical skill, and a deep faith in Christ, I build websites with purpose — clean, functional, and people-first. I believe code can be a craft, and that every project is a chance to serve others, tell a story, or bring a vision to life.
Whether I’m debugging JavaScript during nap time or sketching layout ideas with one hand and holding a toddler with the other, I pour my whole heart into every project. My work is shaped by my values: faith, family, and intentional design.
When you work with me, you’re not just getting a developer — you’re getting a collaborator who truly cares, communicates, and creates with soul.
